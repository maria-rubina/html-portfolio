The Best Movies According to Ruby

My top 3 movies of all time.

Titanic

Titanic (1997), directed by James Cameron, is a romance-disaster film about Jack, a poor artist, and Rose, a wealthy woman, who fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. Their love story unfolds as the ship tragically hits an iceberg, leading to its sinking. Jack sacrifices his life so Rose can survive, blending romance and tragedy in this iconic film.

All Harry Potter Movies

The Harry Potter film series follows the journey of a young boy, Harry Potter, who discovers he's a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry faces various magical challenges and uncovers the dark history of his nemesis, Lord Voldemort.

Across eight films, Harry learns about his own past, hones his magical abilities, and ultimately confronts Voldemort in a final battle to save the wizarding world. The series explores themes of friendship, bravery, and the power of love, becoming one of the most popular franchises in cinematic history.

The Sound of Music

The Sound of Music (1965) is a musical film set in Austria during the late 1930s, following Maria, a lively young nun-in-training, who becomes the governess for the seven children of Captain Georg von Trapp, a strict naval officer. Through her love for music and her free-spirited nature, Maria brings joy and unity to the family, and she and the Captain eventually fall in love. As the Nazis rise to power, the family faces danger and must flee Austria. The film blends romance, music, and the themes of love, family, and courage in the face of oppression, featuring iconic songs like "Do-Re-Mi" and "My Favorite Things."
